About Me

This is what I do.

Hello and welcome to my blog! My name is Santiago, and I'm excited to have you here. In this blog, I'll be sharing my thoughts and experiences on a wide range of topics, including finance, migration experiences, language learning, tech and other stuff.

Whether you're looking for inspiration, advice, or just a fresh perspective, I hope you'll find something here that resonates with you. But before we get started, I wanted to take a moment to introduce myself and give you a bit of background about who I am and what I'm all about. I am a 19-year-old guy from Colombia how is working his way up in Australia, from working in cleaning and demolition to an IT Analyst role.

I'm passionate about personal finance, programming, football and human behaviour, and I've been studying and enjoying those fields for quite a few years now. Ultimately, my goal with this blog is to share some of the most useful and influential data I've had the opportunity to learn throughout this process. I hope that you'll find my posts informative, engaging, and thought-provoking.

Thank you for visiting my blog, and I look forward to connecting with you!
